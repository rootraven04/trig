    Blackjack Hand Decisions

    And speaking of actions, it is the Blackjack hand decisions that make online Blackjack so popular. These choices distinguish each new game round and provide you the ability to influence the game. Depending on the cards you've been dealt, you can make a few distinct hand selections during a round of Blackjack.

    Hit

    You can Hit after obtaining your first two cards, unless you have a Blackjack. If you choose to Hit, the dealer will deal you another card. This procedure can be repeated until you pass out or choose to stand.

    Stand

    You will opt to Stand if you are satisfied with your hand. You will be unable to do anything else after you have chosen this action. You'll have to wait for the other players to make their decisions before the dealer exposes their cards.

    Double Down

    You can Double Down if you believe you have a winning hand with just one more card. Double down is doubling your original bet in return for one more card. When you receive the final card, you will automatically Stand.

    Split

    If you are given a pair, you can choose to Split. Splitting your hand accomplishes this by splitting your hand. Each split hand is dealt one card, and an ante bet is placed on the new hand. After that, the game continues normally.
